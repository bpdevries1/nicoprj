Playwith
=========
playwith tries to work out the structure of a plot, in order to interact with
it. The built-in features include: navigating the data space, identifying and
brushing data points, editing and annotating the plot, and saving to a file.
Interactive controls for parameters (sliders, spinbuttons, combo-boxes, etc) can
be generated automatically. New tools can also be defined.
